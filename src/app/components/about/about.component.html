Who I Am

Hi, I'm Steven! Programming websites doesn't feel like work—it's a part of me. I love bringing great designs and animations to life in projects that people use and that enhance their lives.

I live in Bad Karlshafen, in the Kassel district. My goal is to work remotely so that I have the flexibility to work from Thailand.

I am always eager to learn new things and stay up to date with the latest technologies—this has always been part of my nature. New technology excites me; it feels like being a child receiving a brand-new toy.

Programming brings together many aspects of my life. It combines the analytical thinking I developed from my background in chemistry, the creativity I express in music production, the persistence I built through bodybuilding, and the teamwork skills I gained from gaming. That's why programming doesn't feel like something I just do—it's truly a part of my personality.
